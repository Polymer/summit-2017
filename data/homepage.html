Tobacco Dock. London.

Located in the heart of London's enterprise zone and walking distance from the Tower of London, Tobacco Dock will be home to the second Polymer Summit.

Built in the early 1800's and once home to a warehouse for imported tobacco, the venue is now a thriving event space featuring striking timber beams, industrial ironwork, and brick courtyards.

Events & Amenities

After Hours

Attendees are invited to an after party on Monday, October 17th from 6-10pm.

Child Care

We will offer child care reimbursement to attendees during Polymer Summit. Children aged 3 months to 10 years are eligible. Please note that we have limited availability and may not be able to accommodate all requests.

Prayer Room & Mother's Room

There will be a mother’s and prayer room open on Monday, 17 October from 8:00-22:00 and Tuesday, 18 October from 8:00-18:00. Sign up sheets will be available in front of the room and usage is first come, first served. Please see the registration desk for more information.

Accessibility Accommodations

If you have any accessibility accommodation needs, please email polymer-dev-summit@googlegroups.com or let the staff know when you check in. We will provide live captioning services in the general sessions.
